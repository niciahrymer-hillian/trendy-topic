## How to read this

Because the sample pack holds an equal 60 conversations per country, cross-country *volume* is flat by design — the meaningful signal is the **topic mix within each country** and the **shared global curiosity** questions. Load the full Hugging Face export (see `README.md` and `data/wildchat_country_csv_pack/export_actual_wildchat_by_country.py`) to surface real volume differences.

## Ethics & limits

All analysis runs on safe, PII-redacted summaries; toxic rows are excluded and raw chat text is never sent to translation or voice APIs. See `docs/ethics_policy.md`.
